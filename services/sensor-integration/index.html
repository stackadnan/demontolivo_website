Professional sensor integration services to streamline your project

Whether you need to retrofit industrial equipment with smart sensors, develop a new medical device, or improve supply chain transparency, you have come to the right place. ITRex's embedded system engineers specialize in sensor integration, offering comprehensive services:
Sensor selection & feasibility analysis
Our sensor integration services begin with an analysis of your needs. This allows us to select appropriate off-the-shelf sensors (e.g., temperature, pressure, motion, light, humidity, etc.) considering your deployment, precision, and power requirements. The discovery phase helps avoid delays and costly reworks caused by incompatible or underperforming sensors.
Hardware integration
To enable sensor integrations, we physically connect the selected sensing devices with your hardware using PCBs or connectors. ITRex can also assist you in designing and assembling custom hardware interfaces to support multiple sensors, ensuring compatibility with microcontrollers, processors, and edge devices while improving product scalability.
Sensor data acquisition
ITRex's sensor integration expertise includes configuring sensors to collect data from their surroundings or equipment, enabling analog-to-digital conversions, and implementing real-time data streaming or batch collection protocols. Our sensor data integration services provide quality data for analytics and automation, setting the stage for robust data-driven applications.
Firmware & driver development
Our team is well-versed in firmware and middleware development. We create low-level software to support sensor integrations with cyber-physical systems, including drivers that turn raw sensor signals into actionable insights. We also tweak firmware for high performance and low power consumption, ensuring that the sensors work seamlessly within the system architecture.
Sensor communication
ITRex provides dependable sensor integrations via wired protocols such as I2C, SPI, and UART. Weâ€™re also knowledgeable about wireless connectivity solutions, implementing standards like MQTT, Bluetooth/BLE, Zigbee, Z-Wave, and LoRa/LoRaWAN to connect sensors to cloud platforms and physical devices. Collaborate with us to optimize data transfer rates, reduce latency, and improve system responsiveness!
Sensor calibration & testing
An essential part of sensor integration is fine-tuning them to ensure accurate measurements. To that end, ITRex performs rigorous testing under various operating conditions to detect and correct errors in sensor output. Whether you are developing a critical system, such as solutions for remote patient or industrial monitoring, or you want to cut equipment maintenance costs, you can count on our sensor integration expertise.
Analytics & visualization
Our data consultants and embedded engineers will use bespoke algorithms or AI models to transform raw sensor data into operational guidance. As part of sensor integration, we also offer data visualization services, crafting dashboards and human-machine interfaces to help users make better-informed decisions and enhance their experience.
End-to-end product development
Besides IoT sensor integration, ITRex has the right skills and expertise to manage the entire electronic product development lifecycle, from ideation to deployment, troubleshooting, and ongoing support. We combine hardware, software, and cloud solutions to create complete sensor-based systems and can assist you with testing, certification, and investor pitches.
Emerging tech implementation
We perform advanced sensor integrations, experimenting with state-of-the-art technologies like energy harvesting and biosensing and engineering innovative products for the automotive, healthcare, and utilities sectors. FAQ

What industries can benefit from sensor integration services?
Sensor integration services are valuable in a variety of industries, including healthcare, automotive, agriculture, manufacturing, logistics, and consumer electronics. Sensor integration allows for precise data collection and dependable system performance, whether you are developing a medical IoT device, creating an autonomous vehicle system, or optimizing your supply chain.
How does ITRex ensure the sensors we recommend are compatible with your project?
During the discovery phase, we conduct a comprehensive feasibility analysis. This includes understanding your deployment environment, precision requirements, and power constraints in order to choose a sensor integration strategy that perfectly aligns with your project objectives.
Does ITRex integrate sensors into legacy systems or older equipment?
Yes, we specialize in retrofitting existing systems via sensor integrations. This includes designing custom hardware interfaces, developing compatible firmware, and ensuring seamless integration without disrupting your existing operations.
How do we address data security in sensor integration projects?
To protect your systems from data breaches and unauthorized access, we implement robust cybersecurity measures such as encryption protocols, secure communication standards (e.g., MQTT, CoAP), and regular software updates.
What are the typical timelines for completing a sensor integration project?
Project timelines vary according to their complexity. For example, integrating a single sensor may take a few weeks, whereas advanced sensor integrations in full-scale IoT systems may take several months to complete. We collaborate closely with you to set realistic milestones and ensure timely delivery.
How does ITRex test the performance of sensor-integrated systems?
We conduct rigorous testing under both simulated and real-world conditions, employing tools such as hardware-in-the-loop setup. This includes evaluating sensor integrations for accuracy, dependability, environmental resilience, and compliance with applicable industry standards.
Does ITRex help customers meet industry-specific regulations?
Absolutely. Our team is proficient in regulatory standards such as ISO 26262 for automotive systems, FDA and MDR for medical devices, and GDPR and HIPAA for data privacy. We ensure your sensor integrations meet all necessary compliance requirements.
Does ITRex provide support after the sensor integration project is completed?
Yes, we offer post-deployment support, including troubleshooting, software updates, recalibration, and ongoing system optimization to maintain performance and reliability over time.
What kind of scalability can you expect from our solutions?
Our sensor integrations are built to scale. Whether you intend to add more sensing devices, process larger data volumes, or enter new markets, we ensure that your solution can adapt to changing needs without major overhauls.
How does ITRex use emerging technologies in sensor integrations?
We actively seek out cutting-edge sensor technologies, such as energy-harvesting sensors and AI-enhanced analytics, and incorporate them into our sensor integration services to give your solution a competitive advantage in innovation.
