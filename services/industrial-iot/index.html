Industrial IoT (IIoT) Solutions

Maximize equipment performance, boost quality control, optimize energy usage, and prevent accidents on the factory floor with our Industrial IoT solutions

IIoT solutions we deliver

We help retail and manufacturing and supply chain companies turn their machinery, inventory, and warehouses into a wealth of data. Next, our IoT development team will extract insights from this data, helping you increase throughput while reducing operational costs. Our IIoT solutions know-how includes:
Asset & inventory tracking Equipment monitoring & maintenance Monitor your high-value assets and inventory using inexpensive printed tags, motion sensors, proximity technologies, and computer vision solutions! Your data will be visualized via dynamic dashboards and apps with advanced reporting capabilities. Contact our Industrial IoT company to learn more! The ITRex IIoT development team will aggregate equipment performance data, securely relay it to gateways and cloud servers, and feed it to machine learning models. This way, our IIoT solutions can deliver smart recommendations on overall equipment effectiveness (OEE), maintenance, and potential failures.
Supply chain management Smart factories Our Industrial IoT solutions have got you covered at every stage of your product’s journey through the supply chain — from quality control on production lines to condition monitoring at warehouses and during deliveries. This becomes possible thanks to a powerful combo of sensing devices, application integration, and data analytics tools. From monitoring equipment usage and availability to optimizing gas and electricity consumption by feeding sensor data to AI algorithms, ITRex knows how to transform your production facility into the factory of tomorrow. And if you need an intelligent CCTV system or body-worn sensors for employees’ vital signs monitoring, we can help you, too.

IIoT services we provide

Whether you’re new to Industry 4.0 or seek IIoT project rescue services, ITRex can step in at any stage, filling skill gaps in your internal IT team or acting as a turnkey IIoT solution provider. Check out our IIoT development services:

IIoT consulting
To create an Industrial IoT software solution that benefits your company, our business analysts will put your processes under a magnifying glass and pinpoint areas for improvement. Next, they’ll team up with software architects and R&D unit leads to select a fail-proof IIoT development tech stack and create an implementation roadmap, assessing ROI perspectives early on.
IIoT platform development
As part of our Industrial IoT services, we’ll help you build fully customized IIoT platforms based on Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud. Our Industrial Internet of Things platforms stand out for their robust device and data management capabilities and, unlike most PaaS and SaaS offerings, are tailored to your unique business needs.
Industrial equipment retrofitting
We’ll give your legacy equipment a digital overhaul using custom and off-the-shelf IoT retrofit kits featuring sensors, actuators, and the accompanying software infrastructure, such as on-premises and cloud-based data storage solutions and human-machine interfaces (HMIs). Drop us a line to get started!
IIoT software development
Our Industrial Internet of Things service package includes embedded, cloud-native, web, and mobile application development. So whether you need bare-metal firmware for sensors, a voice assistant living on your machines, or a sleek dashboard democratizing access to operational data, we’re only one email away!

Our IIoT development toolbox

Connectivity & proximity: Bluetooth/BLE, RFID, NB-IoT, LoRa, ZigBee, Sigfox
IoT platforms: Azure Industrial IoT, AWS IoT, IoT Core
Operating systems: FreeRTOS, Nucleus RTOS, AOSP, Embedded Linux
Dashboards: Kibana, Grafana, Tableau, Power BI
Back end: Java, PHP, Python, .Net, Go
Mobile: Swift, Kotlin, Xamarin, Flutter
Sensors: temperature, humidity, pressure, motion, vibration, electricity, O2/CO2

Why build your IIoT solutions with ITRex?

We don’t engineer standalone IIoT products
ITRex will merge your IIoT solutions with any enterprise application and back-office system, whether it’s eProcurement software streamlining equipment parts replacement or company-wide resource planning solutions. That’s how you get a 360-degree view of your processes and operations.
We don’t shy away from hardware design
Unlike many IIoT companies that only build apps, our team can help you design, prototype, and test the hardware components of your Industrial IoT solution — from printed circuit boards (PCBs) to sensors and custom device enclosures.
We don’t promise the impossible
During the project discovery phase, ITRex might learn your machines cannot be retrofitted or a particular connectivity protocol won’t ensure stable data transmission. We’ll tell you that immediately — and come up with a viable alternative if such an alternative exists.
We DO care about your success
Once on a mission to connect your equipment, employees, and workflows, ITRex won’t stop until your objectives are met. By creating an IIoT solution implementation roadmap from the onset, we can thoroughly plan your project, set realistic milestones, and gradually scale your product across other use cases and, ultimately, company-wide.
