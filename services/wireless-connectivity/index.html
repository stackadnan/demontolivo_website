Wireless connectivity solutions

Collaborate with ITRex to develop dependable wireless connectivity solutions optimized for high performance, low latency, and efficient energy consumption

Build reliable wireless connectivity solutions with ITRex

As a software engineering firm with an in-house R&D lab and extensive experience in IoT development and electronics product design, we can keep your devices secure, updated, and connected when and how you need it. To achieve this, we provide the following wireless connectivity development services:
Consulting and protocol selection
Before developing wireless connectivity solutions, we conduct a discovery phase and feasibility studies to assist our clients in selecting the best-fitting data and network protocols based on specific project requirements such as connectivity range, power consumption, data rate, and deployment environments.
Network architecture design
Our wireless connectivity services include network architecture planning and design. Aside from selecting an appropriate architecture pattern (e.g., star, mesh, peer-to-peer) that supports the selected protocols and meets your scalability, redundancy, and communication needs, we will strategically place and configure network nodes and implement security mechanisms such as data encryption in transit and at rest, authentication, and security key management.
Embedded systems design
ITRex embedded system engineers design hardware platforms using readily available microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators with embedded wireless solutions that support the chosen protocol. We will also select and integrate wireless communication system-on-chip (SoC) solutions and pre-certified modules ranging from LoRa to Zigbee and BLE, as well as antennas optimized for specific frequency bands.
Firmware Development
As part of our firmware development services for wireless connectivity implementation, we develop custom drivers that connect communication hardware to operating systems and middleware solutions. ITRex also creates low-power firmware for battery-powered devices, incorporating sleep/wake cycles and utilizing appropriate wireless protocols. Additionally, we enable mechanisms for secure over-the-air (OTA) firmware updates.
Software development for wireless connectivity
Our wireless connectivity expertise includes the implementation and customization of data and network protocol stacks, which ensures that devices communicate effectively with one another. We will also develop software that connects embedded systems to cloud platforms and allows for localized data processing capabilities on devices, reducing latency in industry-specific solutions such as medical and automotive software.
Testing and validation
As quality assurance experts, ITRex will ensure that your wireless connectivity solutions meet the desired range, signal strength, and data rate requirements in controlled and real-world scenarios. We will further advise you on regulatory compliance, safety, and performance certifications, perform vulnerability assessments to close security gaps, and ensure that your network can scale to accommodate additional devices.
Monitoring and maintenance
After implementing wireless connectivity technology, ITRex supports clients on their transformation journey. We can help configure effective tools to monitor network health, data throughput, device status, and performance in real time, leveraging our DevOps and MLOps expertise. Our service offering also includes performance tuning, long-term technical support for connectivity issues, firmware updates, and much more!
Lifecycle management
Aside from providing voice to connected devices, we keep them secure, up-to-date, and fully functional for the rest of their lifetime. ITRex also helps clients add new devices, nodes, or expand coverage with minimal disruption to their existing system. Finally, we can safely decommission connected devices at the end of their lifecycle, removing them from the network and erasing sensitive data.

How does the wireless connectivity development process unfold?
Our wireless technology development process is specifically designed to ensure smooth execution and optimal results.

We begin by identifying your project's specific requirements, technical goals, and business objectives. We conduct a discovery study to identify suitable technologies and embedded wireless connectivity protocols. The ITRex team will then validate and refine our hypothesis through a proof of concept (PoC).

Once the project requirements are clear, we develop high-fidelity prototypes that help speed-test the solution, assess its performance, and gather valuable feedback. Using the insights gained in the previous stage, we begin MVP development, focusing on core features and removing the fluff; this allows you to go to market faster and more effectively manage your budget.

After the MVP goes live, our wireless technology experts will continue to solicit stakeholder feedback for future product enhancements. Whether you want to implement new features, improve the performance of your wireless connectivity solutions, or scale your network to meet your user base and evolving business needs, ITRex has the right skillset and resources to help you on your IT journey!
